Stereotyping

    Have I felt it?

  • When?

  • I'm an artist I am not supposed to be good at math, or organized

  • I'm a woman, so I shouldn't be logical or scientific

  • I'm a believer so I can't be interested in science

  • I'm a woman, so I can't be a tough leader.

  • Science and computer science are for someone else

  • I'm emotional so I can't be logical

  • I'm emotional so I'm bound to be a drug addict

  • I'm impulsive so I can't be organized

  • I'm an artist so I can't make money

  • I'm mixed race so I can't belong anywhere

  • I'm bisexual so I can't be monogamous

  • I'm over 30, so I can't work in technology

    What questions did I ask myself?

  • Am I confirming this belief?

  • Am I giving in to this cultural belief?

  • Am I limiting others like me because I have given in to stereotyping

  • Do I have beliefs that are limiting that I am not aware of?

  • How do I become more aware?

    Did I feel it physically?

  • I used to loathe the things about myself that made me look different

  • I used to feel so much stress that I felt ill when my intelligence was challenged

  • Fear causes stress

  • When I don't take leadership I stagnate

  • Stress and lack of worth leads to insomnia

    What would others feel?

  • They don't strive because what would the point be?

  • Lack of trust because others are judgmental

  • Fear of expression, lack of expression

  • Dreaming small, being stuck

  • Giving int to stereotypes

    How can I help others?

  • Outreach

  • Be an example of a successful: Female-Bi-sexual-Artist-scientist-mixed-race-above-30-believer-programmer

  • Create community support for minority women

  • Be a vocal woman

  • Be a mentor
