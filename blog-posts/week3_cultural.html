My Biggest Problems

  • Getting overwhelmed by how much I need to do

  • Feeling I must be perfect: ---> I put too much pressure on myself --->which creates stress: --->when I am stressed I think less effectively and my body tenses up and I get tired more easily

  • I choose to feel bad: I KNOW I will be unhappy because I have so much to do or I didn't sleep well ---> therefore I am unhappy

  • Negative outlook

  • Doing things and tasks to validate myself instead of doing them to learn, grow and because I Choose to. I then carry a huge burden of never being good enough and having to complete all my tasks in order to assert my value.

  • I feel like breaks and self care are slacking off, I don't deserve rest or sleep!

  • I hate simply listening and not thinking ahead and analyzing while listening to others talk. Perhaps my need to be heard is making me anxious?

  • I have a hard time paying attention to technical auditory tutorials, I get bored, and start thinking ahead or about what I think about what is being said, then I miss things.

  • I can't stand reading very technical blogs and directions! I get so bored and KNOW I will have a hard time remembering the task until I do it myself.

    More Stretching my ways of being

      Improving retention Reading technical blogs

    • Take notes

    • Summarize

    • Explain to others

    • Draw pictures to illustrate idea

      Improving Listening

    • Repeat in my head each word the speaker says

    • Take notes on what they say

    • Concentrate on the sound of their voice, the construction of the sentence, the time it takes them to say it, and body language

    • I will attempt to not analyze or prepare a response

    • I will not interrupt, I will show that I care about what they are saying, and about them by waiting for them to complete their thought even if we are in a rush and I think Know what they are saying.
