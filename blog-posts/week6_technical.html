Instant-ly Variables

why and how to make use an @instance variable

Why?!

  1. We use instance variable to give our object state

  2. We modify the state of only or instance of BabyMonster "Lulu", when we call a method on "Lulu"

  3. Our state is not mutable outside our class

  4. Our instance variables are accessible within in any method in our class, yay!

  5. Our instance state is not viewable outside our class unless we define a class that reveals it

  6. Do you want your bank account info available outside your account login? say on the homepage?
