What scares me most?

I'm nervous about pairing sessions. I am pretty slow at typing, although I am improving. I'm nervous about making my partner frustrated. I'm concerned about communicating my ideas well and compassionately. I'm concerned about how effective pairing will be with two beginners. I'm learning to be beginner. I'm learning how I learn. I am hoping that I will develop strategies for dealing with the frustration and fear. I sometimes fell bad when I can't figure out the answer, I start to feel stupid and then become overwhelmed. I'd like to have more confidence and patience with myself. Why am I putting myself down for not knowing the answer before I have learned it?
